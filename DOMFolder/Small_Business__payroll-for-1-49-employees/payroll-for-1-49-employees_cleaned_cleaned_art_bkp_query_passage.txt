### Small Business Payroll FAQs

**Queries this section answers:**
- How much does small business payroll cost with ADP?

- What are the main benefits of using payroll software vs doing it yourself?
- Does ADP provide payroll services in all 50 states?
- How does ADP handle W-2 and 1099 form filing?

Small business owners commonly have questions about payroll costs, software affordability, and implementation. The cost of small business payroll depends on several factors, including payroll frequency, total number of employees and the specific services needed, with most solutions featuring a per-payroll processing fee. ADP offers an affordable solution starting at $39 per month plus $5 per employee for basic payroll, taxes and compliance support. While some small business owners initially manage payroll themselves, many eventually turn to payroll service providers due to the time requirements and potential costly penalties from mistakes. Running small business payroll involves tracking employee hours, calculating wages and withholdings, distributing wages, depositing taxes with government agencies, filing returns, and sending required tax forms. Quality payroll software typically includes device-agnostic processing capabilities, automated tax calculations and filing, employee self-service options, error-flagging technology, and specialized compliance support. The primary benefits include significant time savings, ensuring accurate and timely employee payments, and allowing business owners to focus on revenue-generating activities. By minimizing payroll errors and automating processes, payroll software can actually save money through reduced labor expenses and avoided tax penalties. ADP provides payroll services in all 50 states and offers a straightforward setup process requiring payroll records and IRS Form 8655 completion. The platform handles hours calculation, deduction processing, wage distribution, tax filing, and secure record storage, with flexible payroll frequency options based on industry and state regulations. Employees can access pay statements electronically where permitted, and employee self-service features allow them to update contact information and manage withholdings. ADP also creates and files W-2 and 1099 forms directly with the IRS on behalf of clients.
